Section 01
The Platform

The GCC Playbook is India's first AI-native GCC intelligence platform. It is live at gcc-playbook.pithonix.ai and free for any company in the world to use. In under ten seconds, it generates a complete, personalised GCC blueprint for any company planning to set up in India.

A company tells the platform their industry, the functions they want to set up, their target headcount, their preferred city, and their timeline. The platform uses real India GCC data to generate a blueprint covering city selection, three-year cost projections, government incentives, entity structure, and a 90-day action plan. Every company that completes a blueprint is actively planning their India entry right now.

Section 06
The Benefit in Practice

The question any specialist firm asks is straightforward: will this actually bring us real work? Here is what the partnership means in practical terms, by category.

⚖️ Legal and Compliance Firms

Every GCC needs a WOS incorporated, board resolutions filed, and FEMA compliance set up before the first rupee moves. You are brought in at Phase 1, the earliest stage, before most other partners. Projects that start in Hyderabad or Bengaluru are likely to have ongoing compliance and contract work for years. The initial incorporation is the start of the relationship, not the end.

🏢 Real Estate and Workspace

City selection in the blueprint is data-driven. When a company picks Hyderabad, you already know their headcount target, timeline, and whether they want managed workspace or a long-term lease. You are not doing discovery calls. You come in with a shortlist that fits the brief Pithonix has already confirmed with the client.

👥 Recruitment and Staffing

The Talent Matcher and Skill Gap agents in JEET identify exactly what roles are needed and when. Your brief from Pithonix includes the role profile, the location, the salary benchmark, and the hiring window. You are not starting from a blank JD. The intelligence layer does the prep work. You do the hiring.

💻 IT Infrastructure

GCCs coming to India need network build-out, hardware procurement, security stack setup, and connectivity from day one. You know the office location, the headcount phasing, and the security requirements because Pithonix has already scoped these with the client. Your quote is based on real requirements, not assumptions.

💰 Banking and Finance

Account opening, payroll setup, FEMA-compliant transfers, and forex are all time-critical for a new GCC. You come in at the entity formation stage with the client already introduced by Pithonix. The relationship starts warm, not cold. The client needs a banking partner who understands GCC structures, and Pithonix has already told them you do.

🎓 Training and L&D

JEET's Learning Path agent builds a personalised training roadmap for every hire from day one. When a client needs structured L&D delivery, you know the workforce profile, the skills gaps identified by the platform, and the training timeline. You are not designing from scratch. You are delivering against a data-backed plan.

The Compounding Value

GCC setups are not one-time engagements. A company that opens a 50-person GCC in Year 1 is likely to scale to 150 by Year 3 and 400 by Year 5. Legal work, real estate expansion, new rounds of hiring, IT upgrades, and compliance renewal all come back around. Partners who deliver well on the first project stay inside the relationship for the lifecycle of that GCC, not just the setup phase.
